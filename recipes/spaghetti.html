Spaghetti

Spaghetti lovers read ahead!

Spaghetti is a simple and enjoyable meal that is perfect for any occasion. It's easy to make and sure to please your family and friends. With a variety of sauce options and the ability to add in additional ingredients, spaghetti is a customizable meal that can cater to any taste or dietary need. Plus, it's a budget-friendly meal that won't break the bank. Overall, spaghetti is a classic comfort food that will always be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 8-10 oz spaghetti noodles
  • 1 jar of your favorite spaghetti sauce
  • Water
  • Salt (for seasoning the boiling water)
  • Optional garnish: grated Parmesan cheese or chopped fresh herbs

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Brown 1 pound of ground beef in a separate pan over medium heat until fully cooked.
  3. Add spaghetti noodles to the boiling water.
  4. Cook the spaghetti noodles for about 8-10 minutes or until they are tender but still firm to the bite (al dente).
  5. Drain the water from the spaghetti noodles using a colander.
  6. Rinse the spaghetti noodles under cold water to stop them from cooking and to remove any excess starch.
  7. Add your favorite sauce to the pan with the cooked ground beef and heat on low heat.
  8. Add the cooked spaghetti noodles to the sauce and ground beef mixture, and stir until the noodles are coated evenly.
  9. Serve the spaghetti in bowls or plates.
  10. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese or chopped fresh herbs, if desired.
  11. Enjoy your delicious spaghetti with ground beef!
