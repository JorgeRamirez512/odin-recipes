Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Melty, crispy, and oh-so-satisfying

Grilled cheese is a simple pleasure that can be prepared in minutes with just a few ingredients. With its ooey-gooey melted cheese and golden-brown crust, a well-made grilled cheese is a delicious and comforting treat that is sure to satisfy any craving. Plus, with endless variations and customizable toppings, it's easy to make a grilled cheese that's perfectly suited to your taste.

Ingredients

  • Bread (sliced)
  • Cheese (sliced or grated)
  • Butter or margarine

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium-low heat.
  2. Spread butter or margarine on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread butter-side down onto the skillet/griddle.
  4. Add cheese (and any desired optional ingredients) on top of the bread in the skillet/griddle.
  5. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter-side up.
  6. Cook the sandwich for 2-3 minutes on each side or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Remove the sandwich from the skillet/griddle and let it cool for a minute before slicing and serving.
