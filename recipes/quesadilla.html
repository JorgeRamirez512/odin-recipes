Quesadilla

Crunchy, Cheesy, and Loaded with Flavor

A quesadilla is a flavor-packed delight that satisfies your craving for something crunchy, cheesy, and savory. The combination of melted cheese, crispy tortilla, and tasty filling makes it a mouthwatering treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, beef, or pork
  • 1/4 cup sautéed onions and peppers
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sautéed onions and peppers, and cook for 2-3 minutes until soft.
  3. Add the cooked chicken, beef, or pork to the skillet, and cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  4. Remove the filling from the skillet and set it aside.
  5. In the same skillet, place one tortilla and sprinkle half of the shredded cheese on top.
  6. Add the filling on top of the cheese, then sprinkle the remaining cheese on top of the filling.
  7. Place the second tortilla on top of the cheese.
  8. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the cheese starts to melt and the bottom tortilla is golden brown.
  9. Carefully flip the quesadilla and cook the other side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  10. Remove the quesadilla from the skillet, cut it into slices, and serve hot.
